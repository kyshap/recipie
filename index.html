Mache Salad with Toasted Hazelnuts and a Citrus Dressing

Notes

Serving Size

Small Bunch

Yield

20 servings

Ingredients

  • Main Dish
  • Mache greens, fresh
  • 6 ounces Hazelnuts
  • 16 ounces Citrus dressing

    • Dressing

  • 1 cup Canola and olive oil blend
  • ¼ cup Sherry vinegar
  • ¼ cup Orange concentrate
  • 2 each Oranges, zest and juice
  • 1 each Lemon, zest and juice
  • 1 tablespoon Sugar
  • Salt, To taste
  • White pepper, freshly ground, To taste

    • Preparation

    1. Gather all the ingredients and equipment.
    2. Lightly toast the hazelnuts and crush with a chef’s knife or the back of a pan.
    3. Zest the oranges and the lemons. Blanch the zest in boiling water for a more delicate flavor.
    4. Juice the oranges and the lemons, or cut into segments and remove seeds.
    5. Combine oil, vinegar, citrus juices, orange concentrate, sugar, salt and pepper.
    6. Just before serving, pick the mache leaf clusters from the roots. In a small bowl with a few hazelnuts and enough dressing to moisten the leaves, turn the mache gently to coat, maintaining shape and structure.
    7. Arrange on plate. Garnish with additional hazelnuts.

    Chef's Note

    As much as possible, salad greens should be dressed à la minute.

    This is especially true with delicate lettuces and herbs.
